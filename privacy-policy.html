Privacy Policy for Chatlith

Last Updated: 08.09.2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chatlith ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Chatlith (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Display name, gender preferences, and behavior preferences for AI interactions
  • Usage Data: Your conversations with AI companions, ratings, and interaction patterns
  • In-App Purchases: Subscription information and payment details (processed by Google Play)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, and device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the App, features used, session duration, and user behavior
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics
  • Liths Data: Your earned and spent virtual currency (Liths) and transaction history

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Play Services: Purchase history, subscription status, and payment information
  • Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics and user behavior patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality and AI conversation features
  • Process AI conversations and generate appropriate responses using OpenAI's technology
  • Track your progress, award virtual currency (Liths), and manage your subscription
  • Manage your subscription and in-app purchases through Google Play
  • Improve the App's performance, user experience, and AI responses
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service
  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of our services

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 OpenAI API

  • Purpose: We use OpenAI's API to generate AI responses and conversations
  • Data Shared: Your conversation messages are sent to OpenAI for processing
  • Data Usage: OpenAI may use this data to improve their services and for abuse prevention
  • Retention: OpenAI may retain conversation data for up to 30 days
  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy/
  • Terms of Service: https://openai.com/terms/
  • Important: We do not control how OpenAI processes your data

4.2 Firebase (Google Cloud)

  • Authentication: User login and account management
  • Database: Storage of user profiles, preferences, usage data, and conversation history
  • Analytics: App usage statistics and user behavior tracking
  • Crashlytics: App crash reporting and error tracking
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Data Location: Firebase data is stored on Google's global servers

4.3 Google Play Services

  • In-App Purchases: Subscription and payment processing
  • App Distribution: App delivery through Google Play Store
  • Play Services: Core Android functionality and services
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.4 Expo

  • Development Platform: App development and deployment tools
  • Analytics: Development and usage analytics
  • Privacy Policy: https://expo.dev/privacy

4.5 Other Open Source Libraries

  • React Native: App framework
  • React Navigation: Navigation components
  • Expo Linear Gradient: UI components
  • React Native Picker Select: UI components
  • Expo Haptics: Device vibration
  • AsyncStorage: Local data storage
  • React Native UUID: ID generation

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase's cloud infrastructure with industry-standard encryption
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure
  • We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information

6. Data Retention

  • We retain your account information and usage data as long as your account is active
  • Account Deletion: You can request account and data deletion at any time by completing our official form here: Account Deletion Request Form. We will process all verified requests within 30 days.
  • We may retain certain information for legal compliance, business purposes, and to prevent fraud
  • Inactive accounts may be deleted after 3 years of inactivity

7. Email Verification

  • Current Status: We do not currently require email verification for account creation
  • This means you can create an account with any email address
  • We cannot verify that you own the email address associated with your account
  • This may limit our ability to provide account recovery services
  • We recommend using a valid email address for account recovery purposes

8. Children's Privacy

Outside North America and Brazil, our App is suitable for users aged 3 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 3. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
In North America and Brazil, our rating is 10+ meaning our App is suitable for users aged 10 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 10 from these regions. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
